Our Story

Homemade chocolates have always tempted me for its taste and pretty packing style and wished to learn its manufacturing process as I believed making it on my own would surely be an exciting task and I was not wrong.

I had a lot of time to invest in any such fruitful activity due to the lockdown so I surfed for various videos of making chocolates at home and my eyes stucked on an online chocolate making classes on instagram which motivated me to learn making chocolates and setup a business for the same. The experiment on various types of chocolates went on few days and a good response from my family helped to make up my mind to start with our instagram page for selling chocolates.

All this began out of excitement and craze of making chocolates on my own. My interest in cooking and baking made me search How To Make Chocolates at Home on You Tube and I tried one of those recepie but it didn't turned out to be good which disheartened me.
